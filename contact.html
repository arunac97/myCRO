Frequently Asked Questions

How long is the free trial?
Our free trial lasts 14 days and includes access to all Professional plan features. No credit card required to start.
Do I need to install anything?
Just add a single JavaScript snippet to your website. It works with any platform including WordPress, Shopify, React, and custom-built sites.
Is my data secure?
Absolutely. We're GDPR and CCPA compliant, with SOC 2 Type II certification. All data is encrypted at rest and in transit.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes. There are no long-term contracts. Cancel or downgrade anytime from your account settings. We also offer prorated refunds.
Do you offer custom enterprise plans?
Yes! Enterprise plans include custom session limits, dedicated CSM, SSO, SLA, and priority support. Contact our sales team to discuss your needs.
